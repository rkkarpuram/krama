
# Privacy Policy for Krama

## 1. Overview

Krama is an educational app designed to teach scientific and mathematical concepts through interactive exploration, guided experiments, and challenges. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 2. Data Collection

Krama accesses the following device sensors **solely for in-app educational purposes:**

- **Accelerometer** – Motion and trigonometry concepts
- **Gyroscope** – Rotational motion and angular velocity
- **Magnetometer** – Earth's magnetic field visualization
- **Light Sensor** – Light intensity and properties
- **Proximity Sensor** – Distance measurement concepts
- **Pressure Sensor** – Atmospheric pressure and altitude
- **Step Counter** – Motion tracking and activity monitoring
- **Rotation Vector** – 3D orientation and rotation visualization
- **Camera** – Overlay canvas drawings on camera view
- **Microphone** – Audio analysis for pitch and frequency concepts
- **GPS** – Geographic coordinates and location-based mapping

## 3. Data Storage & Retention

**Krama does not store any data.** All sensor data is processed in real-time for interactive demonstrations only. Lab reports are displayed in-app but not saved by the app. You can manually copy lab reports as text and save them to your device as you wish. If you do not copy a report, it is automatically destroyed when the mission or activity closes.

## 4. Data Transmission

**No data is transmitted over the internet.** All processing happens entirely on your device.

## 5. Permissions

Krama requests only the permissions necessary for its educational features. You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Krama does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## 7. Future Updates

As Krama evolves, we may add new features and sensors. This policy will be updated accordingly, and changes will be posted here.
